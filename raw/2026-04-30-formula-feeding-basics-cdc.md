# Formula Feeding Basics — CDC Infant and Toddler Nutrition

## Key Points
- Always measure water first, then add formula powder — never reverse the order
- Powdered formula must be mixed with water heated to at least 70°C (158°F) to kill Cronobacter
- Never use illegally imported formula or products from unverified third-party distributors (FDA safety concern)
- Prepared formula: use within 1 hour of feeding start, or within 2 hours if not yet fed
- Refrigerated prepared formula: safe up to 24 hours
- Never reuse formula from a bottle the baby has already fed from (bacterial contamination)
- Opened powdered formula can: use within 1 month; store in cool, dry place (not refrigerator)
- Water source: boil and cool if tap safety is unknown; contact local health department if unsure

## Raw Content
The CDC provides detailed guidance on safe preparation and storage of infant formula to prevent foodborne illness.

**Step-by-step preparation (powdered formula):**
1. Wash hands thoroughly with soap and water
2. Sterilize bottle and nipple before first use; thereafter wash with hot soapy water or dishwasher
3. Measure the correct amount of water into the bottle FIRST
4. Add the correct number of level scoops of formula powder (never pack or heap scoops)
5. Cap and shake well
6. Feed immediately or store according to guidelines below

**Temperature for killing Cronobacter:**
- Cronobacter sakazakii is a rare but dangerous bacteria found in powdered formula
- Preparing with water ≥70°C kills Cronobacter; cool the mixture under running cold water to safe feeding temperature before giving to baby
- Premixed liquid formula does not carry the same risk and does not require heating

**Storage guidelines:**
- Freshly prepared formula (not refrigerated): use within 2 hours of preparation or 1 hour from start of feeding
- Refrigerated prepared formula: use within 24 hours
- Unopened powdered formula: follow expiration date on package
- Opened powdered formula: use within 1 month, store in original container with lid tightly closed in a cool dry place

**FDA Warning:**
- Do NOT use formula sold outside official retail channels or imported without FDA review
- Counterfeit or improperly stored formula may lack key nutrients or be contaminated
