# Breastmilk Storage & Pumping — NHS

## Key Points
- Room temperature: up to 6 hours maximum
- Fridge (≤4°C) back section: up to 8 days (3 days if temperature unsure)
- Ice compartment in fridge: up to 2 weeks
- Freezer (≤−18°C): up to 6 months
- Cool bag with ice packs: up to 24 hours
- Once thawed: use immediately; NEVER refreeze
- After baby drinks: discard within 1 hour
- Never microwave — causes hot spots that burn baby's mouth
- Store at back of fridge/freezer (not door)
- Store in small portions (labelled with date) to minimise waste
- Partners can give expressed milk by bottle at night — allows longer sleep for feeding parent

### Expressing Methods

**Hand Expression**
- Easier in first days/weeks for some; no equipment needed
- Wash hands first
- Cup breast in "C" shape (finger and thumb a few cm from nipple)
- Gently squeeze without sliding fingers over skin
- Build rhythm; alternate between breast areas and both breasts as flow slows

**Manual Pump**
- Cheaper than electric; slower
- Requires more physical effort

**Electric Pump**
- Faster; adjustable suction; sometimes available for hire
- Ensure proper fit — wrong size causes pain and nipple damage
- Build suction strength gradually

### Thawing and Warming

**Thawing:**
- Preferred: leave in fridge to thaw completely overnight
- Quick: place in jug of warm water or hold under warm running water
- Do NOT microwave — creates hot spots
- Once defrosted: use immediately — DO NOT refreeze

**Warming:**
- Warm water (jug or running water) — NOT microwave
- Test temp before feeding (wrist inside test)
- Defrost only what baby will drink

### After Baby Drinks
- Discard any remaining milk within 1 hour
- Do not return leftovers to fridge or freezer

### Containers
- Sterilised hard containers or special breast milk storage bags
- Label with date and volume
- Store in small portions to avoid waste

### Partner Role
Partners can feed expressed milk by bottle during the night, allowing the feeding parent a longer rest period. This supports breastfeeding sustainability without replacing it.
